導入AI技術，實現智慧品質控管

Implementing AI for Smart Quality Control

本專案聚焦於G112類產線的「外觀瑕疵自動檢測」模組，旨在利用先進的AI視覺技術，解決傳統人工檢測的痛點，提升生產效率與產品質量。

This project focuses on the "Automated Visual Defect Detection" module of the G112 production line, aiming to solve the pain points of manual inspection by using advanced AI vision technology to improve efficiency and product quality.

G112類產線：智慧製造的基石

The G112 Production Line: A Cornerstone of Smart Manufacturing

G112類產線整合了多個自動化硬體模組，模擬從物料搬運到檢測分類的完整流程。以下為構成此智慧產線的核心單元。

The G112 line integrates multiple automated hardware modules to simulate the entire process from material handling to inspection and sorting. The following are the core units of this smart production line.

CV輸送帶平台

CV Conveyor Platform

擔任產線的物流骨幹，負責物件的連續傳送，是串連各工作站的基礎。

Serves as the logistical backbone, responsible for the continuous transport of items, connecting all workstations.

雙軸搬運平台

2-Axis Handling Platform

進行精準的水平與垂直移動，用於工位間的物料快速、準確轉移。

Performs precise horizontal and vertical movements for rapid and accurate material transfer between stations.

物件翻轉平台

Object Flipping Platform

將物件進行180度翻轉，以利於攝影機對產品的正反兩面進行檢測。

Flips objects 180 degrees to allow cameras to inspect both the front and back sides of the product.

旋轉檢測平台

Rotating Inspection Platform

提供360度的全方位視角，專為需要無死角檢測的複雜曲面產品設計。

Provides a 360-degree view, designed for products with complex surfaces that require inspection with no blind spots.

6軸手臂平台

6-Axis Robotic Arm Platform

模擬人手進行複雜的抓取、搬運與分類任務，是實現高度自動化的關鍵。

Simulates a human hand to perform complex picking, placing, and sorting tasks, key to high-level automation.

靜態光學系統

Static Optical System

提供穩定、可控的光源與拍攝環境，確保影像品質，是訓練AI模型的理想平台。

Offers a stable, controllable lighting and camera environment to ensure high-quality images for training AI models.

本專案議題 | Our Project's Focus

外觀瑕疵自動檢測

Automated Visual Defect Detection

產品品質是製造業的核心，而外觀檢測是品質控管 (QC) 的第一道防線。我們選擇此議題，因其能直接解決關鍵的產業痛點，帶來最立即且顯著的效益。

Product quality is the core of manufacturing, and visual inspection is the first line of defense in quality control (QC). We chose this topic because it directly tackles a critical industry pain point, offering the most immediate and significant benefits.

電子業 (Electronics): 檢測電路板(PCB)上的焊點缺陷、元件錯位。

Detecting soldering defects or component misplacements on PCBs.

金屬加工 (Metalworking): 找出金屬零件表面的刮痕、凹陷或裂縫。

Finding scratches, dents, or cracks on the surface of metal parts.

紡織業 (Textiles): 辨識布料上的斷紗、色差或織造瑕疵。

Identifying broken threads, color variations, or weaving flaws in fabrics.

食品包裝 (Food & Packaging): 挑出食品中的異物或檢查包裝印刷錯誤。

Sorting out foreign objects or checking for printing errors on packaging.

挑戰與目標：從人工到智慧的跨越

The Challenge & Goal: A Leap from Manual to Smart

傳統人工檢測面臨效率、準確度與成本的巨大挑戰。我們的目標是利用AI技術，建立一個更高效、更可靠的自動化品質控管系統。

Traditional manual inspection faces significant challenges in efficiency, accuracy, and cost. Our goal is to leverage AI to build a more efficient and reliable automated quality control system.

傳統人工檢測痛點

  • ✗
    效率低下：檢查速度有限，易成產能瓶頸。

    Low Efficiency: Limited inspection speed becomes a production bottleneck.

  • ✗
    錯誤率高：受人員生理、心理狀態影響，標準不一。

    High Error Rate: Inconsistent standards due to human factors like fatigue.

  • ✗
    成本高昂：需投入大量人力、訓練與管理成本。

    High Cost: Requires significant investment in labor, training, and management.

  • ✗
    無法全檢：多採抽檢，存在品質風險。

    Inability for 100% Inspection: Relies on sample checks, posing quality risks.

導入AI解決方案目標

  • ✓
    提高檢測速度：實現遠超人力的即時高速檢測。

    Increase Speed: Achieve real-time, high-speed inspection far exceeding human capabilities.

  • ✓
    提高準確率：維持穩定客觀標準，準確率達99%以上。

    Improve Accuracy: Maintain stable, objective standards with over 99% accuracy.

  • ✓
    降低生產成本：顯著降低對人工的依賴。

    Reduce Cost: Significantly decrease reliance on manual labor.

  • ✓
    實現數據化品管：記錄瑕疵數據，作為優化生產依據。

    Enable Data-driven QC: Collect defect data to optimize production processes.

我們的AI解決方案

Our AI Solution

我們選擇了業界領先且成熟的技術組合，以確保系統的性能、穩定性與未來的擴展性。

We have selected a combination of industry-leading, mature technologies to ensure system performance, stability, and future scalability.

深度學習: YOLO

Deep Learning

選擇理由：兼具速度與準確率，能實現即時偵測，完美契合產線需求。

Reason: Balances speed and accuracy for real-time detection, perfectly fitting production line needs.

影像處理: OpenCV

Image Processing

選擇理由：功能強大且為業界標準，能確保影像品質，最大化模型效能。

Reason: Powerful and the industry standard, ensuring optimal image quality to maximize model performance.

邊緣運算: NVIDIA Jetson

Edge Computing

選擇理由：低延遲的邊緣運算確保即時反應，且易於整合、數據安全性高。

Reason: Low-latency edge computing ensures real-time response, easy integration, and high data security.

AI-Powered AOI Workflow

為克服人工檢測的限制，我們開發了一套高速自動化的AOI系統。

To overcome the limitations of manual inspection, we developed a high-speed, automated AOI system.

產品進站 (Product Entry)

產品進入旋轉檢測平台。

Products enter via the rotating inspection platform.

影像擷取 (Image Capture)

5MP工業相機搭配同軸光源，擷取高清、無眩光的影像以確保特徵清晰。

5MP industrial cameras with co-axial lighting capture high-definition, glare-free images to ensure feature clarity.

AI辨識 (AI Detection)

Jetson Orin Nano運行客製化訓練的YOLOv8n模型，瑕疵檢測mAP達99.2%，推論時間15ms。

A Jetson Orin Nano runs a custom-trained YOLOv8n model, achieving 99.2% mAP for defect detection with an inference time of 15ms.

自動分類 (Automated Sorting)

系統透過TCP/IP向PLC發出信號，控制六軸手臂對NG品進行分類。

The system signals a PLC via TCP/IP, controlling a 6-axis arm to sort NG (No-Good) products.
